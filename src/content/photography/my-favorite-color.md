There are only two seasons in my hometown, winter and summer. Spring is very short, as short as a week, so I've never gotten the full picture of spring. It was only upon my footsteps treading the grounds of UVA that a revelation dawned upon me. My favorite color wasn't just a standard hue like [_Red_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R_rUYuFtNO4), _Blue_, or _Green_. It is the vibrant mosaic of _Spring_ !!!
